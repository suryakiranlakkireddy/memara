Daily Reminders

Customizable Reminders: up daily reminders for medications, appointments, meals, and activities. These can be scheduled by caregivers or loved ones and include voice notes or pictures for easy recognition.
Alerts: Reminders can be set to repeat daily, weekly, or monthly with gentle notifications.

Memory Book

Photo Albums:Create and organize photo albums with captions or voice descriptions. Each photo can have a brief description to help trigger memories.

Life StoryA section to store important life events, family history, and significant milestones, narrated by loved ones

Video Memories:Store short video messages from family members that can be replayed to provide comfort and familiarity.

Location Tracking and Safe Zones

GPS Tracking:Real-time location tracking that caregivers can access, ensuring the person with dementia is safe.

Safe Zones:Set up safe zones (like home or a familiar park), with alerts sent to caregivers if the user leaves these areas

Emergency Button:A one-touch button that can send an alert to a designated caregiver with the person’s location.

Simplified Communication

One-Touch CallsA simplified interface allowing the user to make calls to pre-set contacts (e.g., family members, caregivers) with a single touch.

Video Calls: Easy-to-use video calling feature with large buttons and clear instructions.

Text-to-Speech:Convert text messages to speech, helping users understand incoming messages.

cognitive excercises

Games and Puzzles: Simple cognitive games like memory matching, word searches, or number puzzles designed to stimulate the brain and maintain cognitive functions.

Music Therapy:Access to a playlist of familiar songs or calming music, which can help reduce anxiety and improve mood.

*Daily Quizzes:Short, personalized quizzes based on the user’s life or interests, keeping their mind engaged.

Routine Builder

Daily Schedule: Visual daily routine planner with icons and voice prompts, helping the user maintain structure in their day.

Activity Suggestions: Suggest activities based on the time of day, weather, or user’s interests, such as “Time for a walk” or “Let’s read a book.”

Caregiver Portal

*Shared Access:Caregivers and family members can access the app remotely to update reminders, track location, or communicate with the user.

Health Monitoring:Integrate with wearables to monitor vital signs, activity levels, and sleep patterns, providing insights to caregivers.

Emergency Contacts: Store a list of emergency contacts that can be accessed quickly if needed.
